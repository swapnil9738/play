About Us

SKSPM is established in Nov. 2017 as per Society Registration Act 1860 and Mumbai Public Trust Act 1950. Registered to Charity Commissioner of Maharashtra at Latur. Institution focuses on welfare of deprived through various ways like Awareness Education Camps, Training, Employment and direct help of Food, Clothing and Shelter.

History

Founder saw that in Latur district there are so many Women, Children and Youth who are facing many problems such as Health, Education, Empowerment, Agricultural, Livelihood, Awareness etc. Also, we see many people in our community who needed help and hence started this adventure.
